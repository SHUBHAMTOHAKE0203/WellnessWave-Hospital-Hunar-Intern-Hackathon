Patients and Families

Welcome to Our Hospital

At our hospital, we are dedicated to providing compassionate and comprehensive care for our patients and their families. Our goal is to ensure that every patient receives the best possible care in a supportive and comfortable environment.

We offer a range of services and support to help you navigate your healthcare journey. Whether you are a patient or a family member, we are here to assist you with information, resources, and guidance every step of the way.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the visiting hours?

Our visiting hours are from 9 AM to 8 PM daily. Please check with the specific department for any variations in visiting hours.

How can I get a copy of my medical records?

You can request a copy of your medical records through our Health Information Management department. Please fill out the request form and provide valid identification.

What support services are available for families?

We offer various support services including counseling, family support groups, and financial assistance programs. Please contact our social services department for more information.
