Visiting Hours

We encourage family and friends to visit their loved ones during the following hours:

  • General Wards: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • ICU: 12:00 PM - 6:00 PM
  • Maternity Ward: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Visitor Guidelines

To ensure the comfort and safety of all patients, we ask visitors to adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Limit the number of visitors to 2 per patient at a time.
  • Wash hands before and after visiting.
  • Avoid visiting if you have symptoms of illness.
  • Respect the privacy and needs of other patients.
