Day Surgery

Providing safe and efficient care for same-day surgeries.

What is Day Surgery?

Day surgery, also known as outpatient surgery, allows patients to undergo surgery and return home on the same day. Our dedicated team ensures a smooth and safe experience from start to finish.

Preparing for Your Surgery

  • Follow all pre-surgery instructions provided by your doctor.
  • Do not eat or drink anything after midnight before your surgery.
  • Arrange for someone to drive you home after your surgery.
  • Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing on the day of your surgery.

What to Expect on the Day of Surgery

1. Check-In

Arrive at the hospital at the scheduled time to complete the check-in process.

2. Pre-Surgery Prep

Our nursing staff will prepare you for surgery, including administering any necessary medications.

3. Surgery

Your surgeon will perform the procedure in one of our state-of-the-art operating rooms.

4. Recovery

After surgery, you will be monitored in the recovery area until you are ready to go home.

Post-Surgery Care

After your surgery, it's important to follow these guidelines to ensure a smooth recovery:

  • Follow your doctor's post-surgery instructions carefully.
  • Take prescribed medications as directed.
  • Keep the surgical site clean and dry.
  • Attend follow-up appointments as scheduled.
